# Games & controls

## Tic‑Tac‑Toe
- **Mode**: 2-player local or vs AI.
- **Controls**: click a cell to place your mark.

## Connect 4
- **Mode**: 2-player local or vs AI.
- **Controls**: click a column arrow to drop a disc.

## Nim
- **Mode**: 2-player local or vs optimal AI.
- **Controls**: choose how many to take (1..max) and click **Take**.

## Hangman
- **Mode**: single-player.
- **Controls**: type a letter and press Enter or click **Guess**.

## Dots and Boxes
- **Mode**: 2-player local.
- **Controls**: click an edge to draw a line.
- **Rules**: completing a box scores 1 point and you **play again**.

## Sudoku
- **Mode**: single-player.
- **Controls**: type digits 1–9 into empty cells. Invalid moves are rejected.

## Nonogram (Picross)
- **Mode**: single-player.
- **Controls**:
  - Left click: toggle filled cell
  - Right click: toggle X mark

## Crossword
- **Mode**: player + editor.
- **Controls**:
  - **Play**: type letters into cells.
  - **Edit**: right click toggles blocks; type solution letters; edit across/down clues.
